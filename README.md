# crime-data-analysis
Exploratory data analysis on real-world crime datasets using Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib and Seaborn. Uncovering patterns and insights through data cleaning, transformation and visualisation.

Analyzing Crime in Los Angeles!!

Los Angeles, California, attracts people from all over the world, offering lots of opportunities, not always of the good kind!

In this project, I am serving as a data detective, supporting the Los Angeles Police Department (LAPD) in analyzing crime data to guide how they should allocate resources to protect the people of their city!
